BLAZE ENCHANTER - BOOK + HEAD TRACKING
=======================================
Two changes:

1) HEAD TRACKING (BlazeEnchanterBlockEntity.java)
   The blaze head now looks toward the player, exactly like the Blaze Burner
   (uses AngleHelper.deg(-Mth.atan2(dz,dx))-90 and chases headAngle). The book
   uses the same headAngle, so they rotate together.

2) FLOATING BOOK (BlazeEnchanterRenderer.java)
   Renders the real vanilla BookModel with the special blaze_enchanter_book texture,
   floating above the cage, bobbing and rotating with the head. Page flutter included.
